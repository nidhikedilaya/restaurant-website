Welcome to our Spice Haven.

Spice Haven is an Indian restaurant known for its authentic and flavorful cuisine. The menu features a wide range of traditional Indian dishes, including aromatic curries, tandoori specialties, and freshly baked naan. With its warm and inviting ambiance, Spice Haven offers a delightful dining experience, showcasing the rich culinary heritage of India. Whether you're a fan of spicy fare or prefer milder flavors, this restaurant caters to all palates with its diverse and delectable offerings.

About Us

Discover our restaurant story

Spice Haven was founded by Nidhi Khadabadi, inspired by her childhood in Bangalore, where she learned the art of blending spices from her mother. After moving to the city, she missed the flavors of home and decided to open a restaurant that would share authentic Indian cuisine with a wider audience. In 2004, Spice Haven opened its doors, committed to using traditional techniques and importing spices directly from India. The restaurant quickly became known for its exceptional food and warm, welcoming atmosphere. Today, Spice Haven is a beloved community hub, celebrated for its dedication to quality and authenticity, offering a taste of Indiaâ€™s rich culinary heritage.

Misal Pav

Misal pav is a dish from the Indian state of Maharashtra. It consists of misal and pav. The final dish is topped with farsan or sev, onions, lemon and coriander. It is usually served hot with bread or rolls toasted with butter and buttermilk or dahi and papad.

Frequently asked questions

What are the login hours?

Our restaurant is open for orders from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Monday through Sunday.

How do I get my refund?

To request a refund, please contact our customer service team via phone or email within 24 hours of your purchase. Provide your order number and details, and we will process your refund promptly.

How long it will take food to arrive?

Delivery times typically range from 30 to 45 minutes, depending on your location and the size of your order.

Does your restaurant has both veg and non veg?

Yes, our menu includes a wide variety of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes to cater to all dietary preferences.

What is cost of food delivery?

The delivery fee is $3.99 for orders within a 5-mile radius. Additional charges may apply for longer distances.

Who is eligible for pro membership?

Our pro membership is available to all customers. Members enjoy exclusive benefits such as discounts, priority seating, and special event invitations. Sign up on our website or in-person at the restaurant.

Paneer Tikka

Paneer tikka or Paneer Soola or Chhena Soola is an Indian dish made from chunks of paneer/ chhena marinated in spices and grilled in a tandoor. It is a vegetarian alternative to chicken tikka and other meat dishes. It is a popular dish that is widely available in India and countries with an Indian diaspora.
